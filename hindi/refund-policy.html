Refund Policy

Effective Date: 15-Sep-2024

Thank you for choosing Smart Income Hub. We strive to ensure satisfaction with our subscription services. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are granted. By subscribing to our services, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Refund Eligibility
1.1 Request Period

Refund requests will be considered only if raised within 24 hours of the successful payment. Any request made after this 48-hour period will not be eligible for a refund.

1.2 Refund Request Process

To request a refund, subscribers must contact Smart Income Hub via email at smartincomehub.care@gmail.com or through the designated refund request form on our website. The request must include the subscriber's details and proof of payment.

2. Refund Conditions
2.1 Full Refund

If a refund request is made within the 48-hour period, a full refund of the payment will be processed. This includes the entire subscription fee paid by the subscriber.

2.2 Non-Refundable Period

Requests made after the 48-hour period will not be entertained, and the subscriber will forfeit any right to a refund.

2.3 Processing Time

Once a refund request is approved, Smart Income Hub will process the refund within 7 business days. The refund will be issued through the original payment method used during the transaction.

2.4 Refund Limitations

Refunds are applicable only to the subscription fee and do not cover any additional costs or fees incurred by the subscriber. No refunds will be issued for any services rendered or tasks completed during the subscription period.

3. Subscription Cancellation
3.1 Cancellation Request

Subscribers wishing to cancel their subscription must notify Smart Income Hub in writing via email at smartincomehub.care@gmail.com. Cancellation will be effective as of the date the written notice is received.

3.2 No Refund for Partial Periods

Cancellation of a subscription does not entitle the subscriber to a refund for any unused portion of the subscription period. Subscribers are responsible for managing their subscription and ensuring that refund requests are made within the specified period.

4. Exceptions
4.1 Discretionary Refunds

Smart Income Hub may, at its discretion, grant refunds outside of the specified 48-hour period under exceptional circumstances. Such decisions are made on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

4.2 Service Issues

If there are significant issues with the service provided, subscribers should contact Smart Income Hub immediately. While this policy outlines standard refund conditions, we aim to address and resolve any service-related concerns promptly and fairly.

5. Contact Information
5.1 Contact Information

For any questions or to request a refund, please contact us:

Email: smartincomehub.care@gmail.com
