Refund Policy

Effective Date: 15-Sep-2024

Smart Income Hub ko chunne ke liye dhanyawaad. Hum apni subscription services ke saath satisfaction sunishchit karne ke liye prayaas karte hain. Yeh Refund Policy un conditions ko outline karti hai jinke tehat refunds diye jaate hain. Hamari services ko subscribe karne par, aap neeche diye gaye terms ko manne ke liye sahmat hain.

1. Refund Eligibility
1.1 Request Period

Refund ki requests sirf tabhi consider ki jayengi jab yeh successful payment ke 24 ghante ke andar ki gayi ho. Is 24 ghante ke baad ki koi bhi request refund ke liye eligible nahi hogi.

1.2 Refund Request Process

Refund ki request karne ke liye, subscribers ko Smart Income Hub ko email karna hoga smartincomehub.care@gmail.com ya hamari website par designated refund request form ke zariye. Request mein subscriber ki details aur payment ka proof shaamil hona chahiye.

2. Refund Conditions
2.1 Full Refund

Agar refund ki request 24 ghante ke andar ki gayi ho, toh payment ka full refund process kiya jayega. Ismein subscriber dwara diya gaya poora subscription fee shaamil hai.

2.2 Non-Refundable Period

24 ghante ke baad ki requests entertain nahi ki jayengi, aur subscriber kisi bhi refund ka haq kho dega.

2.3 Processing Time

Jab refund ki request approve ho jaye, Smart Income Hub refund ko 7 business dinon ke andar process karega. Refund original payment method ke zariye diya jayega jo transaction ke dauran istemal hua tha.

2.4 Refund Limitations

Refunds sirf subscription fee par lagu hote hain aur subscriber dwara kiye gaye kisi bhi additional costs ya fees ko cover nahi karte. Subscription period ke dauran kisi bhi services ya tasks ke liye koi refunds nahi diye jayenge.

3. Subscription Cancellation
3.1 Cancellation Request

Subscribers jo apna subscription cancel karna chahte hain unhe Smart Income Hub ko email ke zariye likhit roop mein notify karna hoga smartincomehub.care@gmail.com. Cancellation tab effective hogi jab likhit notice prapt hota hai.

3.2 No Refund for Partial Periods

Subscription cancel karne par subscriber ko kisi bhi unused portion ke liye refund ka haq nahi milega. Subscribers ko apne subscription ka management karna hoga aur ensure karna hoga ki refund requests specified period ke andar ki jaye.

4. Exceptions
4.1 Discretionary Refunds

Smart Income Hub, apne discretion par, specified 24 ghante ke period ke baahar exceptional circumstances ke tehat refunds de sakta hai. Aise decisions case-by-case basis par liye jaate hain aur yeh guaranteed nahi hote.

4.2 Service Issues

Agar service pradan ki gayi hai mein significant issues hain, toh subscribers ko turant Smart Income Hub se sampark karna chahiye. Jabki yeh policy standard refund conditions ko outline karti hai, hum kisi bhi service-related concerns ko turant aur fair tareeke se address karne ka prayaas karte hain.
